📖 DATA STORY 

The project focuses on analyzing an e-commerce dataset to understand sales performance, trends, and business behavior.

The analysis includes:
- KPI evaluation (Revenue, Orders, Quantity, AOV)
- Category-wise performance
- Monthly sales trends
- Order status distribution
- State-wise revenue contribution

The findings highlight patterns in customer behavior and business performance.

---

📊 Key Insights
- A few categories contribute the majority of total revenue  
- Sales vary across months indicating trends  
- Most orders are successfully delivered  
- Revenue is concentrated in specific states  

---

📈 Hypothesis Testing

Hypothesis:
A small number of categories contribute most of the total revenue.

Analysis:
Category-wise revenue comparison shows that the "Set" category generates significantly higher revenue than others.

Conclusion:
The hypothesis is supported by the data.

---

💡 Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing categories to maximize revenue  
- Improve performance in low-revenue regions  
- Reduce cancellations through better operational strategies  
- Utilize seasonal trends for planning and marketing
